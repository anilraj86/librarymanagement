Progressive Library Vision

The library will be a one stop learning venue by providing various library information resources and services.

Progressive Library Mission

The village Library commits itself to provide its readers community with essential and appropriate services, required facilities, and balanced collection of materials and resources necessary in meeting the current and future needs of the villagers informational, and personal requirements. It assumes a pivotal role in the character development through its commitment to achieve success and efficient delivery of knowledge in various aspects of Govt's instructions and public service.
